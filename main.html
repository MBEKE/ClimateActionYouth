WHO WE ARE

The climate Heroes is a club registered club at the Technical University of Kenya. It is made up of youth determined to make a contribution in the effort to achieve the United Nations SDG 13, Climate Action Through sensitization, informative webinars and community outreach programs, the club aims to take small but consistent actions to make our planet earth a better place to be .
